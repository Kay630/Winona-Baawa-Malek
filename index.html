Professional Work

Arlington County Department of Human Services

Human Services Specialist

In my role at Arlington County DHS, I work directly with families and individuals navigating complex social service systems. My responsibilities include conducting comprehensive assessments, developing individualized service plans, connecting clients with critical resources, and providing ongoing case management support.

I specialize in working with diverse populations, ensuring culturally responsive services that honor each person's background and circumstances. My work encompasses crisis intervention, resource coordination, and advocacy for vulnerable community members.

Key Areas of Impact

Community Programs & Initiatives

Throughout my career, I have contributed to various initiatives focused on family stabilization, youth services, senior support programs, and emergency assistance coordination. I am passionate about building bridges between community resources and those who need them most.
